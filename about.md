<h1>About Kenney Best Ball</h1>

<section class="origin-story">
    <h2>Our Story</h2>
    <p>Kenney Best Ball began in 1999 as a way to honor William A. Kenney, our family's beloved patriarch who passed away too soon from a heart attack. What started as a small family gathering has grown into an annual tradition that brings together family, friends, and the community for a day of golf and giving back.</p>
    
    <p>For over 25 years (with a brief pause during COVID), we've hosted this scramble golf tournament to raise money for causes close to our hearts. From supporting the Home for Little Wanderers (William's charity) to the Pan-Mass Challenge, to installing portable defibrillators in local businesses after one saved a family member's life, each year's beneficiary reflects our family's journey.</p>
</section>

<section class="family-legacy">
    <h2>A Family Tradition</h2>
    <p>Today, Kenney Best Ball is organized by the "2K" generation—William's grandchildren—led by Elle Mahaney, with support from her uncles Rich and Jim Kenney. What began as a simple golf outing has become a cherished tradition that spans four generations of the Kenney family.</p>
    
    <p>The tournament is about more than golf. It's about:</p>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Family:</strong> Reconnecting with cousins, aunts, uncles, and friends</li>
        <li><strong>Memory:</strong> Keeping William's spirit alive through action</li>
        <li><strong>Community:</strong> Supporting causes that matter to our family and community</li>
        <li><strong>Fun:</strong> Enjoying a beautiful day on Cape Cod with people we love</li>
    </ul>
</section>

<section class="tournament-format">
    <h2>Tournament Format</h2>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Scramble:</strong> All skill levels welcome! Each player hits, then the team plays from the best shot.</li>
        <li><strong>Shotgun Start:</strong> All teams tee off at the same time from different holes.</li>
        <li><strong>Contests:</strong> Longest drive, closest to the pin, putting contests, and more!</li>
        <li><strong>Raffle:</strong> 50/50 drawing with exciting prizes.</li>
        <li><strong>Dinner & Awards:</strong> Celebrate the day with food, drinks, and recognition.</li>
    </ul>
</section>
